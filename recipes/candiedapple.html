Candied Apples

Description

This recipe is for apples covered in a thin sugar coating, a traditional treat in the Fall.

Ingredients

  • 30 small apples
  • 5 cups white sugar
  • 1/2 cup golden syrup
  • 1 1/2 cups mineral water
  • 2 teaspoons black currant syrup

Steps

  1. Place wooden sticks into each apple on the stem side.
  2. In a heavy saucepan, mix together the water, sugar, and golden syrup. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to medium.
  3. Remove the sugar mixture from the heat. Stir in the black currant syrup and dip all of the apples.
